# OlympianOrder — Roles

| id | who | tier | seats | how you get it |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `athena, apollo, artemis, hermes, ares, hephaestus, dionysus, aphrodite` | the newcomers | `OLYMPIAN` | 1 | claimed — gated on **essence**, so the veterans of the war are the ones who can afford a throne |
| `aphrodite` |  | `OLYMPIAN` | 1 | **only if EraOfCreation's sky was actually cut.** No Creation jar → the sea was never opened → she isn't there. Nothing crashes. |
| `mortal` | Mortal | `MORTAL` | 500 | no gate, no cost. Set `default-role: mortal`. |

> A power missing from a role's `powers` list **does not exist** — `PowerService` checks it
> before firing. See [Powers](https://github.com/Crew-co/Mythos/wiki/Powers).
