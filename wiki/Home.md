# OlympianOrder

**Three brothers divide the world by lot. Olympus is raised. Mortals appear, and start asking for things.**

Story #3 — the era **`olympian-order`**, which hands on to **`the-abduction`**.

```kotlin
compileOnly("net.crewco:mythos-addon-api:0.1.0")
```

## The mechanic worth knowing

**The chapter that changes the server.** Mortals arrive — 500 seats, no gate — and setting
`claiming.default-role: mortal` turns a hundred spirits into a hundred *people*.

**The lots are drawn blind.** Zeus doesn't *pick* the sky; he draws it. And a god's powers now only work
**inside the domain he drew** — Zeus cannot throw a thunderbolt with a roof over his head, Poseidon on dry
land is a large angry man with a fork, Hades is nothing in daylight. The shape of the most powerful being
in the world was decided by what his hand closed on in a helmet.
